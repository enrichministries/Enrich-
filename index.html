Equipping the Saints

Worship Workshops

Tools to equip you spiritually and musically. Our workshops focus on two main pillars: Worship Leadership and Worship Music.

30 Hours Duration

Worship Leadership Course

A study on the biblical, theological, and practical aspects of Christian worship. Attention is given to leadership issues: nurturing people, working with musicians, strategic planning, and resourcing ministry in the local church context.

Objectives

  • Define true Christian worship as integral to daily life.
  • Understand culture, music, and worship relationships.
  • Practical aspects of building and nurturing worship teams.

Course Outline

1. Worship Vision
2. Lifestyle of a Worshiper
3. Engaging the Spirit Realm
4. What does a worship leader do?
5. Spirit, Word & Worship
6. Building Local Ministry
7. Worship and Warfare
8. How to choose songs?
9. What happens when we play?
10. Emerging Paradigms
30 Hours Duration

Worship Music Course

A deep dive into music theory, melodies, chords, progressions, and beats. Learn to be dynamic and pragmatic while approaching any song for worship.

Course Outline

Fundamentals & Scales

Introduction, Formation of Major Scale, Understanding Minor Scales.

Chords & Harmony

Triads (Major, Minor, Aug, Dim), Sevenths, Suspended, Complex Chord Formations.

Chord Progressions (Roman Numeral)

Natural Formation, Analysis (I-IV-V, I-vi-IV-V7, I-ii-IV-V, etc.), Technical Terms.

Rhythm & Application

Time Signatures (2/4, 4/4, 3/4, 6/8), Chord Colors, Playing Songs.

Our Founder

Rev. Dr. Sunny Prasad

Rev. Dr. Sunny Prasad is the president of 'Enrich Worship Music Academy', a ministry that specializes in catering to the needs of the local church in the areas of worship and music.

He is an ordained minister of the Assemblies of God, serving as the 'Youth and Worship Coordinator' for the Central District Council of South India Assemblies of God (CDSIAG Karnataka & Goa).

Apart from teaching the 'Worship Leadership' course at different Bible Colleges, he specializes in conducting Worship Team Workshops and developing resources for worship ministry. These include audio and video lessons, handbooks for musical instruments, songbooks, and chord charts for worship songs.

He holds a 'Doctorate of Ministry' from the Center for Global Leadership Development (SABC). He resides in Bangalore with his wife Sunitha, who is a 'Family Life Educator'. They are blessed with two beautiful daughters, Sthuthi and Shreya.
